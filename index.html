Bio

Born on November 11th, 1994 in Dnipro(UA). Got basic education at Dnipro's General (elementary, middle and high combined) School #22. In 2012 went to Odessa(UA) to get musical education at Odessa National College of Arts and Culture. Graduated in 2016.

The same year started the mandatory military service period (18 month) in Lviv(UA). Served as a gunner in Ukrainian National Guard (Military Police). Carried out escorting and guarding defendants and convicts in the courtrooms as part of the guard. Was transferred to the reserve in December 2017.

In 2018 moved back to Odessa, worked as freelance musician doing gigs and cruse ship contracts. With the start of global COVID-19 pandemic changed occupation. Starting from July 2020 became member of Periodix.Net team as a lead generation specialist. In March 2021 got the position of Junior Front-End Developer in the same company.

From September 2022 entered GISMA University of Applied Science to complete Bachelors Degree of Software Engineering.

From March 2023 started working as Full-Stack Software Engineer (Working Student) at PERFACCT GmbH located in Potsdam (Brandenburg, Germany).
